## \## ISMS Scope

## 

## \### 1. Context and Boundaries

## 

## The Information Security Management System (ISMS) is defined for \*\*'SecureSoft Solutions, Inc.'\*\* and covers the information assets, people, processes, and technology used to deliver the \*\*"SecureApp Mobile Development Platform."\*\*

## 

## \### 2. Physical and Organizational Boundaries

## 

## \* \*\*Physical Location:\*\* The primary corporate office located at 123 Tech Drive, Silicon Valley.

## \* \*\*Organizational Units:\*\* Development, Quality Assurance (QA), and IT Operations teams directly supporting the SecureApp Platform.

## \* \*\*Excluded Areas:\*\* Human Resources, Finance, and Marketing departments (except where they process platform billing data).

## 

## \### 3. Service Scope

## 

## The ISMS covers all activities related to the lifecycle of the SecureApp Mobile Development Platform, including:

## 

## 1\.  Software requirements gathering and design.

## 2\.  Source code management and version control.

## 3\.  Cloud-based application hosting (AWS/Azure environment).

## 4\.  Data backup and restoration processes for customer data.

## 

## \### 4. Key Stakeholders

## 

## \* \*\*Internal:\*\* Employees, Management, ISMS Steering Committee.

## \* \*\*External:\*\* Customers, Regulators (GDPR, HIPAA), and Cloud Service Providers.
