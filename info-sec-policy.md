## \## Information Security Policy

## 

## \### 1. Policy Statement

## 

## SecureSoft Solutions, Inc. is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets critical to the SecureApp Mobile Development Platform. We commit to establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an ISMS in accordance with the requirements of ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## 

## \### 2. Management Commitments

## 

## \* \*\*Risk Management:\*\* We commit to an annual formal risk assessment and the application of appropriate security controls based on the risk treatment plan.

## \* \*\*Compliance:\*\* We will comply with all applicable legal, statutory, regulatory, and contractual requirements related to information security.

## \* \*\*Resource Allocation:\*\* We commit to providing adequate resources (financial, personnel, and technological) for the effective operation and maintenance of the ISMS.

## \* \*\*Training and Awareness:\*\* All personnel will receive mandatory training on security policies and procedures relevant to their roles.

## 

## \### 3. Policy Review

## 

## This policy shall be reviewed annually by the ISMS Steering Committee or following a significant organizational or technical change.
